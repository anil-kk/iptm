About Us

The Bible

We Believe every word in the Bible is inspired by God

Salvation

We Believe that through personal acceptance of Jesus Christ by faith, asking Christ to forgive our sins, a person becomes a Christian, and our relationship with God is established. The acceptance of Christ assures us that Christ comes into our life (Ephesians 1:13-14); Our sins are forgiven (Colossians 1:14); We begin a new life with Christ (2 Corinthians 5:17); We are assured of eternity in heaven (John 11:25)

The Trinity

We Believe God is one and is the only one God. (Deuteronomy 4:35; 32:29; Isaiah 45:14; 46:9; Exodus 20:3). The trinity means that, within the unified essence of the Godhead, there are three distinct persons who are God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We Believe Jesus is the Son of God who became man in the divine-human form of Jesus Christ (John 1:1-18). Fully God (John 10:30), and at the same time fully man (Philippians 2:7-8). His death on the cross was the answer for mankind’s sins (Romans 3:23-26; 1 Cor. 15:3). And on the third day, He physically rose again. (Matthew 28:6; Romans 1:4).

Holy Sprit

We Believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a life pleasing to God (1 Corinthians 6:19; Galatians 5:22, 23; Ephesians 5:18).

Visions

God has blessed us with an outpouring of Church attendees. God has led us to a piece of land we were able to purchase to build a larger church facility to accommodate the 1800+ Church members. By God’s Grace, the church will continue to grow year after year. The new location is the center of two villages that does not know of Jesus. What a great opportunity to open the hearts and eyes of the people of those two villages by bringing the Word of God to them and to continue sharing the Gospel to all of Tenali and the surrounding villages. Many people are accepting Jesus Christ as their personal Saviour, and growing their relationship with God. It is the will of God, how he is using Pastor Bhanu to be able to meet the needs of the community, providing God’s Word, meals, clothing, and other necessities to the people. Glory all to God! We Believe that we are called with a holy calling to walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit, and so to live in the power of the indwelling Spirit of God through our walk of faith. God call us to follow Him, to preach the gospel; what is to come, and believing that God works all things together for good. (Act 16:9-10; 1 Chronicles 17:15-18; Daniel 2:28-29; Romans 8:28-29)

Church

We Believe that the church is the body of Christ, of which Jesus Christ is the head. The church is made up of a body of believers who have been called out from the world, which was formed on the day of Pentecost (1 Corinthians 12:12, 13; Acts 2:1, 42). The head of the church is Jesus Christ (Colossians 1:18).
Our Mission

Our main mission is to remain true to God first and foremost, and: To work together for the Kingdom of God. To Glorify God by spreading His Word, and Ministering to all castes in the area villages of Tenali. Teaching the non-believers all about Christ Jesus our personal Savior. To provide a church of worship and prayer, to spread the gospel, love, peace, joy to people of Tenali and it surrounding towns villages. To grow devoted followers of Christ.

Church History

IN THE BEGINNING… Following God’s calling, Pastor Bhanu took his wife Parvathi and their two children planted a church where God showed him. Starting out, Pastor Bhanu rented part of a shop using partitions in the shop, Pastor held church services weekly. On the 22nd of December 1996, Bhanu Prasad received a building and started a church with 16 members. Meanwhile he was wondering how they will run the church and who will come. The owner of the building was a church member and he was also wondering how the church would survive. He had a vision from God, “That the Church was not started by Bhanu Prasad, but it was started by “Me”. I have just planted a seed and I will make it a giant tree.” Then the owner shared the vision with Pastor Bhanu, and from that time God worked through Bhanu to bring Christ to the people in the surrounding villages. In 2004 the church building was purchased by Pastor Bhanu, and 60-80 people have been receiving Christ year after year. Praise God!

Pastor Bhanu Prasad Anumolu

Pastor Bhanu Prasad has been married to his beautiful bride Parvathi since 1992. Bhanu has a passion for helping his community, and bringing Christ to all the area villages. Bhanu has dedicated his life to serving God. God has blessed Pastor Bhanu and Parvathi with a son (Ashok), and a daughter (Kavitha). Both children are now young adults, and after numerous prayers on guidance for their children, God provided for them, the funding for Ashok and Kavitha to attend college. Both are planning on becoming doctors so they can help the community as their father and mother. Bhanu was born to a well-settled Hindu farming family on the 6th of October 1971, in Tenali, India. He grew up living a life of immorality, enjoying the world with his friends. During this time he found no peace, until one day he was led to church, and on his first visit all of the church members received him with love. This was the first thing that attracted him to church. This impacted him enough to attend church regularly. Bhanu received peace and started attending church regularly. In the process God gave him the gift of visions, filled him with the Holy Spirit, and at the age of 18 he received Christ as his personal Savior. He received forgiveness from the Lord and God strengthened him. Bhanu was called with this Bible reference: Isaiah 44:21-22. He wept and prayed to God, “I don’t know anything and I don’t have any Biblical training, I have lived an immoral life, and I have no character, no knowledge.” God told him, “I will use you mightily and I will strengthen you.” So he asked God where to start the church, and God showed him an area in the middle of Hindu families. Bhanu’s Testimony: It started with his mother’s vision. Bhanu had a good relationship with his mother. One day his mother had a vision from God. God told her that her son will be God’s servant. She fasted and prayed, then shared this vision with her son. And at the age of 18 years old, Bhanu accepted Jesus Christ as his personal Savior. Soon after, Bhanu received a vision from the Holy Spirit of him strengthening others in Christ. This day he became a servant of God. Although Bhanu did not have the pastoral education, he followed God’s call, which confirmed the vision his mother had years before. In 1992, Bhanu married Parvathi, a beautiful Hindu young lady. They started their family soon after. By 1995, His faith and belief was strengthened during his growing relationship with God. The Pastor at his church suddenly passed away and without training a replacement, the church had no leader. A member of the Church started to lead the people of the church; however, the people quickly saw he was not the true one meant to lead the church. Meanwhile, God spoke to Bhanu and God said “You will lead the Church”. Bhanu then asked God, where am I to go lead your Church. God told Bhanu to take his family and go, God showed him a Church to lead. So Bhanu took his family and 5 other families who also believe Bhanu was true to God and meant to lead a Church as they had the same vision and trusted God. Bhanu shared to the people and started to work in the place God spoke to him about. Praying and fully trusting God’s plan, Bhanu was led to a land with a building already on this land. On the December 22, 1996, Bhanu started God’s Church with 16 members. Meanwhile he was wondering how they will run the church and who will come. The owner of the building was a church member and he was also wondering how the church would survive. He had a vision from God, “That the Church was not started by Bhanu, but it was started by Me. I have just planted a seed and I will make it a giant tree.” Then the owner shared the vision with Bhanu, and from that time he received a burden to bring souls to the Lord. This land was located in a village full of Hindu people. Bhanu knew that God wanted him to plant a seed to the Hindu people, and trusted God to bless the land and place. After 1 ½ years, 185 people came to know God as He promised. In 2004, Bhanu had met with a friend and fellow Pastor Harry Gomes; Pastor Harry prayed with Bhanu and he told him that Bhanu will greatly be used by the Almighty God in Tenali. He will be the first in Tenali. Bhanu was asked to partner with Harry Gomes ministries to partake in crusades where 50 thousand people attended from surrounding villages to hear the word of God. Also this same year, a church building was purchased by Pastor Bhanu, and 60-80 people have been receiving Christ year after year. God spoke to this land owner and he leased the land and the building to Bhanu. Since then, God has blessed the Church Pastor Bhanu was leading, and the church members were too receiving the blessings from Christ, people were getting married, families were starting, people were getting jobs. It was evident that God was definitely blessing the church and the people. In 2007, Pastor Bhanu was led to start another Immanuel Prayer Tower Church in the Guntur District. This Church now holds 300 members. The need and demand was there for Christ, so Bhanu was led to start another Church in another village. On Sunday services, Pastor would travel city to city to lead the services. Pastor prayed for guidance from God. Pastor Bhanu then started to train a Pastor to take over one for him at the Gunther District. And in 2008, Pastor was led yet again to open another Church in a remote village; however, this one location was too small to accommodate the large amount of people attending there. He prayed to God and asked Him what to do. Pastor Bhanu met with Bishop Suresh Kumar (President of Harvest India), and explained this situation of this small church being unable to accommodate a large crowd for services. After speaking with Bishop Suresh, praying and then Bishop Suresh leased him his land. Now all the churches were growing every year. Praise God. Pastor Bhanu, followed God’s calling, planting the seed in different villages and seeing the growth of people of all castes to accept Jesus Christ as their Savior. God blessed Bhanu with the passion to take care of God’s people. Pastor Bhanu is currently praying for land to purchase to build a larger church to accommodate many; to help the community with jobs; to build an elderly home for the homeless elderly people; and building a children’s home.

Parvathi Anumolu, Pastor’s Wife

Following along side her husband Pastor Bhanu, Parvathi has committed herself to serving the Lord. She also has the passion to serve the Lord God by helping the community with weekly meals, daily fellowship at believers homes, providing clothing to the needy, helping the congregation families. Their generosity has become known within the area villages. After marrying Bhanu in 1992, they started a family. God Blessed them with a daughter “Kavitha”, and a son “Ashok”. Parvathi continues serving the Lord, and helping her husband with his ministering to the community, meeting the needs of the people, with future hopes of starting a women’s ministry, and as the congregations continues to grow, adding an elderly and a children’s home. Parvathi was comes from a family who once believed in the Hindu Gods. She was not a believer of Jesus when she married Bhanu. Although Bhanu was not a Hindu when they got married, He witnessed to Parvathi, telling her about Jesus. Bhanu loved Jesus and believe in the one true God. Since Bhanu worshiped Jesus, Parvathi worshiped many Hindu Gods, leaving no peace in the house. When God called Bhanu to be His servant and to follow His call, Parvathi had no choice but to follow her husband. As God was working through Bhanu, Parvathi started to see all the wonderful and amazing miracles that only God could be doing. When she saw Bhanu praying for the people and she noticed good things (blessings) started happening to the people. People of the community were being provided for, healed, prayers were being answered. She began to be obedient. Observing all that God has done for the community. Then Parvathi started to hear God talking to her too. She then started to pray to God and God was giving her peace within. She started to feel the Holy Spirit working in her and she started to believe and forget the Hindu Gods. Parvathi didn’t know how to lead, but God showed her how to help her husband Pastor Bhanu. They both grew in faith and took the guidance of God. Parvathi chose to become obedient to God, and He started to work on her heart. When the Hindu community rejected Bhanu and Parvathi, they didn’t give up, they believed and trusted God to help and provide for them. There were times she felt she was being tested,, especially during some life obstacles, but she remembered God’s promises. Parvathi began to continuously pray to God, and waited patiently for God’s answer. God’s love has never failed. God has blessed Bhanu and Parvathi beyond anything they ever imagined. Providing a way for Kavitha and Ashok to attend the University so when they are finished with their studies, they will come serve the community too. Parvathi and Bhanu both believe this was all God’s plan. The whole family has become servants of God. Giving everything up to God, in His hands. Parvathi’s faith has strengthened as she continues her walk with the Almighty God.
