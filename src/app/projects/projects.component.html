Womens Group

Ministry Work

There is so much poverty in India. Many of the families have only one parent and some believe it is in the best interest of their children to give them to orphanages. These are called “Poverty Orphans”. Children growing up in an orphanage, grow up surrounded by strangers, they are vulnerable to abuse, and neglect We at IPTM believe the children need to be with family. Without the support of family or church community, most children growing up in a orphanage can become lost and struggle with adult life once out of an orphanage. We believe children need a positive and caring family environment. When a family in despair comes to IPTM, Pastor does all that he can through prayer and guidance from God for the family. IPTM will provide: ♥ Food ♥ Clothing ♥ Housing assistance ♥ Contacts for jobs. By providing them jobs, they will regain their dignity and provide for their families. Never being separated from their children. With your help IPTM would be able to provide more means for the families in need and hopefully in the near future, can create support group programs for the families and promoting family care. Please contact us for more information on how you can help.

Building Project

In Tenali, Andhra Pradesh, India, a village lacks the basic necessities of love, drinking water, sandals, medicine, and food. In this village we are building a pillar of hope. The church’s primary function is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with and make new disciples in this remote corner of the world. God has blessed us with an amazing out-pour of church attendees. People usually travel several kilometers from surrounding villages and towns to participate in the Church Sunday services and other usual activities. With our Church continuously outgrowing our current Church sites, we have prayed for new land, and God led us to a fruitful piece of land to build a larger church facility to accommodate 1800+ Church members. Praise God! With the church continuing to grow each year, the need for a larger church facility is also evident. Now with having to build a new church facility to accommodate all church members, will require many funds, which we don’t have the means to build our new facility. Pastor is seeking Father God’s guidance in this matter. We now have a piece of our own land and are praying for the funds needed to build the new church facility on the new land, and providing a place for the community.
